Frequently Asked Question

  • Why i chose Graphic designing?
    Graphic design is a form of visual communication that connects people to products and ideas. As a graphic designer, you’ll use technical skills, creativity, and imagination to create value, evoke emotions, and develop understanding through design1. Whether you’re designing a small flyer or a large advertising campaign, every project has the ability to make a difference. If you’re passionate about creativity and visual storytelling, a career in graphic design might be the perfect fit for you!
  • What Is Graphic designing?
    Graphic design is the art of creating visual content using elements such as typography, images, symbols, and colors to convey a message to an audience1. It involves creativity, innovation, and lateral thinking, whether using manual or digital tools. Graphic designers play a crucial role in shaping how we perceive information through visual communication.
